🧪 How to Test:

  • Step 1: Enable Fake Filler in Page Controls (⊘ icon)
  • Step 2: Click on any credit card field above
  • Step 3: Right-click → "Fill This Field" OR press Ctrl+Shift+F
  • Step 4: Verify fake card data appears (format: XXXX XXXX XXXX XXXX)
  • Step 5: Check browser console (F12) for detection logs
  • Step 6: Test "Fill Entire Form" to fill all fields at once
  • Step 7: Test contenteditable elements (Stripe-like) for modern payment forms

✅ Expected Results:

  • Card numbers: 16-digit format (XXXX XXXX XXXX XXXX)
  • CVV/CVC: 3-digit code (123)
  • Expiry dates: MM/YY format
  • Console shows: "[Fake Filler] Detected field type: creditCard"
  • All field types (input, contenteditable, role=textbox) work correctly
  • No "invalid field" warnings on card fields
